ABOUT PRODUCT

We present premium-quality products: snacks made from the finest ingredients. Our products are processed using Pure Coconut Oil to deliver both flavor and health benefits. Enjoy the crunchiness of Kepok Banana Chips, the richness of Chocolate Bars, and the savory taste of cashews, almonds, and peanuts. Every bite is a perfect harmony of top-notch quality and unforgettable flavor.

OUR PRODUCTS

Banana Chips

Our banana chips are made from selected Kepok bananas and processed with pure coconut oil without any added preservatives. This process results in chips that are savory, crispy, and rich in authentic natural flavors. Every bite delivers a delightful taste experience that keeps you coming back for more. The perfect snack choice for any occasion.

Banana Crunchy

Banana Crunchy is made from fresh, tree-ripened bananas and cooked with pure coconut oil, without added sugar or preservatives. Naturally crispy, absolutely delicious, and guaranteed to leave you wanting more.

Shuka Chocolate

From Cocoa Beans to Chocolate. The production process includes receiving fermented cocoa beans, roasting, separating cocoa beans from their shells, grinding, mixing, hardening, tempering, molding, cooling, drying, packaging, and distribution. Our premium products include Milk Chocolate, Chocolate with Cashews, Chocolate with Strawberries, Chocolate with Almonds, 60% Vegan Dark Chocolate, 70% Dark Chocolate, and 90% Dark Chocolate.

Peanut

Our nuts are made from the finest Bali peanuts, renowned for their quality and distinctive taste. Hygienically processed, our nuts come in two delicious flavor variants: Original and Onion Flavor. Every bite is packed with irresistible goodness that will keep you coming back for more.

Cashew Nut

Our cashews are roasted in an oven without oil and free from preservatives, offering a naturally savory, authentic flavor with a delightful crunch.

Almond Nut

Our almonds are roasted in an oven without oil and free from preservatives, delivering a naturally savory and crunchy taste while being rich in numerous health benefits.

Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO)
